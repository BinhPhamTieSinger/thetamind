Welcome to ThetaMind

ThetaMind is a next-generation, AI-powered platform designed to make learning mathematics intuitive, engaging, and personalized. Our mission is to transform math education from a challenge into an adventure.

Getting Started

To begin your journey with ThetaMind, you first need an account. You can create one using a traditional email and password or, for convenience, sign in instantly using your Google account.

  1. Navigate to the Register page.
  2. Choose your preferred sign-up method.
  3. Once logged in, head over to the Tools page to see the available learning paths.

Core Features

Learning Paths

Our core learning experience is built around gamified Learning Paths. For each major topic, like Algebra, we provide a visual graph of interconnected concepts. You start at the beginning and unlock new lessons and quizzes as you prove your mastery.

AI Tutor

Every lesson, quiz, and explanation on ThetaMind is powered by a sophisticated AI. This ensures that the content is not only accurate but also tailored to be easily understandable. When you get a quiz answer wrong, the AI provides constructive feedback to help you understand your mistake, not just see the right answer.

Gamification

We believe learning should be fun. By representing topics as a "skill tree," you get the satisfying feeling of leveling up. Watching the nodes light up as you complete them provides a clear, visual representation of your progress and mastery.

Your Account

Your account is your personal hub. The Dashboard page will show your statistics, including your accuracy on different topics and your overall progress. All your completed nodes in the learning paths are saved to your account, so you can always pick up right where you left off.
